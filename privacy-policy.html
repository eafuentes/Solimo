Solimo Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 9, 2026

Solimo is designed for children ages 3–8 and does not collect or sell personal information. We respect family privacy and comply with children's privacy laws.

Information We Collect

  • None. Solimo does not collect personal information from children or parents.
  • Progress data (like age band and daily streak) is stored locally on the device only.

How We Use Information

Any data stored on the device is used only to personalize the learning experience (age level, daily streak, and sound preferences). This data never leaves the device.

Third‑Party Services

Solimo does not use third‑party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Online Interactions

Solimo does not include links to external websites or social media within the child‑directed experience. The app is safe for kids and free of ads.
